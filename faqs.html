FAQs
Questions:
  1. What is the rate plan associated with the SIM in the package?
  2. How can I activate my SIM?
  3. How can I exercise the right of withdrawal if I decide to return the purchased product?
  4. My product does not work, what can do?
Answers:
  1. The SIM from 5 euro contained in the package is configured with the tariff plan according to TIM. Subsequent changes to the tariff plan or to the available options can be made at the TIM Sale point at which you have to go for SIM activation.
  2. Products purchased on SHOP always have a SIM Card TIM inactive. Upon receipt of the product, you can go to the nearest point of sale and request activation of SIM Card TIM. In case of purchase of SIM Card TIM presenting malfunctions, you can request a replacement SIM Card TIM by going by an Authorized TIM shop. If the paper proves to be defective, it will be replaced free of charge. In case of damage, you can request a replacement.
