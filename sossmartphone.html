SOS Smartphone
SOSsmartphone is the TIM service to all customers, or Rechargeable Subscription, you have to receive specialized, personalized technical assistance for the management of your smartphone or tablet.

Thanks to SOSsmartphone service you can talk to an expert who will give you all the advice and "premium" service that you require and that above will be able to remotely connect and operate directly on your device.


The services that will be given for SOSsmartphone are:

  • For data transfer support in case of change phone: example transfer phonebook, photos, text messages
  • Consultancy in the use of advanced features of the smartphone
  • Specialized assistance on advanced configurations of the smartphone and integration into your home network
  • Manage and synchronize your media
  • Support to ensure maximum security
  • Conservation and protection of your data
  • Transfer of content
  • Update of your smartphone
  • Performing check-ups
Service available for:
  • iPhone 6S
  • Samsung Galaxy S7
  • Huawei P9
